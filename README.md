## Project Overview

**Organization name:**
STELLAR 

**Brief History**

The games store website started out as a passion project between a few friends. 

As passionate gamers (especially PC gamers), we noticed a lack of PC platforms that distribute games and even less that offer a great service. 
    
We became motivated to create our own platform to capitalize on this opportunity present and hopefully give the gaming community a platform they can rely on 
    
Platforms such as the Epic Games store and Microsoft Store do not offer a wide variety of games, which we wanted to improve with our store. 
    
Good customer support is also something not present on platforms such as the Epic Games store, which we will prove on by making customer satisfaction our priority.
    
The growing of our team took a long time and we encountered many hardships. 
    
One of the challenges being finding like-minded people who are as determined as we are in making this platform as good as it can be.  
    
Our guiding principles in improving/maintaining STELLAR involves; prioritizing the constant improvement of user and developer experiences, preservation of older games as well as adaptation of platform specific games onto PC and providing an affordable experience 

**Mission and Vision Statements**

- Creating a space that prioritizes a positive user and developer experience 
- Adapting older video games that were only accessible through specific platforms (such as PlayStation) and making them available to play on PC 
- Making it easier for new developers to get exposure for their games 
- Incentivizing developers to optimize their games to create an environment where people with lower-end hardware can still be a part of the platform
- Making a more affordable experience for the average gamer 

**Target Organization**

Individuals with a PC, Desktop or Laptop that are interested in playing video games 

## Website Goals and Objectives 

**Website Goals**

- Selling and distribution of online games 
- Building the reputation of STELLAR as a business organization 
- Preservation of older games 
- Facilitating the careers of future games developers 

**Key Performance Indicators** 

- High website traffic
- Profits made from selling games 
- Interest of the website shown on social media platforms

## Website Analysis 

**Website Strengths** 
- Prioritizes the consumer and developer for an overall better experience 
- There is a high number of optimized games for lower end PCs 
- There is a variety of games available on the store 
- New/smaller developers have found great success and exposure on our store 
- Most games on our website are affordable, with frequent discounts presents 

**Website Weaknesses**

- Since our focus is on providing games that are optimized for lower-end hardware, we don’t have a lot of games with spectacular looking graphics
- The website does not have a lot of games from popular, well-established publishers 
- Developers have expressed concerns over excessive discounts on their games 
- We don’t have the needed number of employees to manage operations 

**Areas of Needed Improvement**

- Allowing for more games that are exclusive to higher-end hardware to increase the number of games that look spectacular 
- Forming connections with more established publishing companies, to host more of their games on our site 
- We need to find a middle ground between the discounts offered by our platform and treating our developers fairly 
- We need to increase our workforce
  
## Website Features and Functionality 

**Homepage**

- There is a button (named “Explore”) that is present and when clicked, takes you to the product page.
- This feature was added in order fast track the user to the store 
- There are multiple sections (Popular, Discounted and Newer Games) present that each have multiple games cards, with buttons that will expand the games descriptions (when JavaScript is added) and images when clicked take you to their respective individual games' pages.
- The user can also scroll through the games of each section.
- These features were added to provide a first-time user with a snippet of the games offered on our platform  
- There is a community section on the page that has a button (named “Join Our Community”) that takes the user to the sign-up page.
- This feature was added to entice the first-time user to sign in and create a profile on our platform 

**About us page**

- There are cards that describe the different departments that STELLAR consists of.
- This feature was added for better visual presentations of the information 

**Contact page** 

- There is a form present on this page that takes your first name, last name, email as well as any important messages that you have, and when the submission button is clicked sends all that information to STELLAR.
- A representative will then respond to your message, and support will be provided.
- This feature was added to facilitate the support of users and developers alike  

**Enquiry page**

- An FAQ section is present, and the reason behind it was to help the user tackle surface level problems.
- The feature involves having a dropdown option that provides you with answers to each question 
- There is another form that takes your information as well as a feature that categorizes your enquiry and the reason behind this feature was to help organize all the enquiries that we get to make it easier for our support team do their job 

**Products page**

- There is a search bar feature present.
- The reason why this feature was added is to allow the user to look for specific products 
- There is a feature that will be added that allows the user to change the layout of the games on the page (when JavaScript is added).
- The reason behind this is to give the user a greater level of choice on how the games are displayed on the platform 
- There are cards that are classified using different game categories.
- When clicked, the feature takes you to a separate page where games that fall under the same categories are displayed together.
- The reason behind this feature is to allow the user to browse through games with the same category 
- There is a feature that will be added that will allow the user to save games later.We added this feature to give the user the capability of wish listing games 
- Clicking the image cards of the games will take you to the individual game pages that has a description of the game and the PC specs needed
