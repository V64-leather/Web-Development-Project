Batman Arkham City

Developer: Rocksteady Studios

Publisher: WB Games

Released: 7 Sep 2012

Batman: Arkham City builds upon the intense, atmospheric foundation of
Batman: Arkham Asylum, sending players flying through the expansive
Arkham City - five times larger than the game world in Batman: Arkham Asylum - the
new maximum security "home" for all of Gotham City's thugs, gangsters and
insane criminal masterminds. Featuring an incredible Rogues Gallery of Gotham
City's most dangerous criminals including Catwoman, The Joker, The Riddler, Two-Face,
Harley Quinn, The Penguin, Mr. Freeze and many others, the game allows players to
genuinely experience what it feels like to be The Dark Knight delivering justice on
the streets of Gotham City.
