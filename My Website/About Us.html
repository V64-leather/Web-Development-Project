Organisational History

Terrain started out with a group of 5 friends deciding out of a deep passion for video games to create an online space to sell them. We obviously were inspired by Steam, which is the most successful online pc game store ever made with no real competitors. We decided to dip our toes in the water because we saw an opening that wasn't really being capitalized on. Our dream was a game store that gave a better experience to the consumer and the developer than what was available, maybe even served as competition for Steam. Even though this is still the beginning, we have grown to a team of over 30 people and are looking forward to all that we can bring to the space.

Mission and Vision Statements

  • Create a space that prioritizes user and developer experiences
  • Making old video games only available on previous console generations more accessible on PC
  • Making it easier for new developers to get more exposure
  • Incentivizing developers to optimize their games if possible and without ruining the intended experience of the game so that customers with older PCs or less powerful PCs are still able to be apart of the gaming experience
  • Making the gaming experience more affordable to the average person, for example by discussing with developers to offer discounts for their product

Team Members

Terrain is the platform it is today thanks to our team of 30+ members. These wonderful individuals satisfy roles for support, maintenance, leadership, etc to ensure that Terrain continues to prioritize and satisfy developer and consumer needs. Here is a breakdown of the departments that our team consists of:

Leadership Team(2 members)

Everything concerning Terrain as a platform is overlooked by this department. They are in charge of making critical decisions that align with the goals of the platform and ensure continued growth

Development Team(9 members)

They are in charge of the platforms building, security, functionality and etc. The maintaining and various performance updates of the platform are up to this team.

Design Team(4 members)

One of our points under Vision Statements talks about prioritizing user and developer experiences, this team is responsible for bringing that vision to life. Which is reflective/revealing in our platforms user friendly nature and pleasing aesthetic.

Products Team(5 members)

The games/content that you see on our platform has been checked and verified beforehand, to ensure that it is a serviceable product. This team is in charge of this moderation, are the ones pushing the featured games on the home page and also create sections for the games on the products page.

Marketing and Community(4 members)

This team works to bring even more members to our platform and continue to grow. They bring more attention to Terrain through our social media and interacts with the gaming community which improves our standing in the community as a trustworthy, consumer-friendly option for gamers.

Customer Support(4 members)

Whether it be account recovery, needing to change a password or applying for a refund, our support staff has you covered. They are responsible for giving you as smooth of an experience as possible. Even accepting of just general inquiries about our platform.

Administration Team(2 members)

This team handles the innerworkings of the business such as; finances, coordination and just the general internal operations of the platform.
