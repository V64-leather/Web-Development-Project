Organisational History

The games store website started out as a passion project between a few friends. As passionate gamers (especially PC gamers), we noticed a lack of PC platforms that distribute games and even less that offer a great service. We became motivated to create our own platform to capitalize on this opportunity present and hopefully give the gaming community a platform they can rely on Platforms such as the Epic Games store and Microsoft Store do not offer a wide variety of games, which we wanted to improve with our store. Good customer support is also something not present on platforms such as the Epic Games store, which we will prove on by making customer satisfaction our priority. The growing of our team took a long time and we encountered many hardships. One of the challenges being finding like-minded people who are as determined as we are in making this platform as good as it can be. Our guiding principles in improving/maintaining STELLAR involves; prioritizing the constant improvement of user and developer experiences, preservation of older games as well as adaptation of platform specific games onto PC and providing an affordable experience

Mission and Vision Statements

  • Creating a space that prioritizes a positive user and developer experience
  • Adapting older video games that were only accessible through specific platforms (such as PlayStation) and making them available to play on PC
  • Making it easier for new developers to get exposure for their games
  • Incentivizing developers to optimize their games to create an environment where people with lower-end hardware can still be a part of the platform
  • Making a more affordable experience for the average gamer

Team Members

STELLAR is the platform it is today thanks to our team of 30+ members. These wonderful individuals satisfy roles for support, maintenance, leadership, etc to ensure that STELLAR continues to prioritize and satisfy developer and consumer needs. Here is a breakdown of the departments that our team consists of:

Leadership Team (2 members)

Everything concerning Terrain as a platform is overlooked by this department. They are in charge of making critical decisions that align with the goals of the platform and ensure continued growth

Development Team (9 members)

They are in charge of the platforms building, security, functionality and etc. The maintaining and various performance updates of the platform are up to this team.

Design Team(4 members)

One of our points under Vision Statements talks about prioritizing user and developer experiences, this team is responsible for bringing that vision to life. Which is reflective/revealing in our platforms user friendly nature and pleasing aesthetic.

Products Team(5 members)

The games/content that you see on our platform has been checked and verified beforehand, to ensure that it is a serviceable product. This team is in charge of this moderation, are the ones pushing the featured games on the home page and also create sections for the games on the products page.

Marketing and Community(4 members)

This team works to bring even more members to our platform and continue to grow. They bring more attention to STELLAR through our social media and interacts with the gaming community which improves our standing in the community as a trustworthy, consumer-friendly option for gamers.

Customer Support(4 members)

Whether it be account recovery, needing to change a password or applying for a refund, our support staff has you covered. They are responsible for giving you as smooth of an experience as possible. Even accepting of just general inquiries about our platform.

Administration Team(2 members)

This team handles the innerworkings of the business such as; finances, coordination and just the general internal operations of the platform.
